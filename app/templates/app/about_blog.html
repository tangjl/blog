About the blog

Welcome to "In Plain English", a blog where the author, Jordan Tang, is here to explain various web development related concepts or terms that are often hard to grasp for beginners who are just starting to get their feet wet in web development. While you can Google most of the concepts discussed here, the aim of this blog is to explain them in ways that anybody (at least an English speaker) could understand. Often times, in my experience, I just want a simple, straight-forward explanation for a specific concept to get started. But more often than not, you get very abstract and difficult to follow explanations from your research. So this blog is here to explain things in plain English! Just look at it as a way for me to give a very high-level understanding of things without having to wrack your brain rifling through dozens of articles on a given topic.*

Anyways, feel free to take a look around, leave a comment, a suggestion, or any requests you my have. Hope this blog proves useful and have fun learning!

*Note: you should always do additional research on top of what is presented here. You should never rely on one single source of information, and remember this is a basic explanation of sometimes complex concepts.
